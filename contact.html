Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are your delivery hours?

We deliver Monday to Sunday from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Same-day delivery is available for orders placed before 2:00 PM.

Do you deliver to my area?

We serve major cities across Kenya. Contact us with your location to confirm delivery availability in your area.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, cash on delivery, and bank transfers. Payment details will be provided when you place your order.

How fresh are your products?

We source directly from local farmers and suppliers daily. All products are fresh and meet our quality standards before delivery.
