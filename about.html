Our Story

Founded in 2019, Score & Spar Foodstuffs began as a small family business with a simple mission: to provide fresh, quality food products to families across Kenya. What started as a local grocery store has grown into a trusted name in the foodstuffs industry.

Our commitment to quality, freshness, and customer satisfaction has been the driving force behind our growth. We work directly with local farmers and suppliers to ensure that every product meets our high standards before reaching your table.

Today, we serve thousands of families across Kenya, delivering fresh produce, quality groceries, and exceptional service right to their doorsteps. Our mission remains the same: fresh food, happy families.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our growth story

2019

The Beginning

Score & Spar Foodstuffs was founded with a small store and big dreams. Started serving the local community with fresh produce and quality groceries.

2020

Digital Expansion

Launched online ordering and delivery services to better serve our customers, especially during challenging times. Expanded our product range significantly.

2022

Community Growth

Reached 500+ regular customers and established partnerships with local farmers. Introduced same-day delivery service across major towns.

2024

Today & Beyond

Now serving 1000+ families across Kenya with 50+ product varieties. Continuing to innovate and expand our services for better customer experience.
